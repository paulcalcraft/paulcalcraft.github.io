ByeTwitterBots

Report and block the latest wave of twitter mention bots in one click.

⚠️ I've only tested this myself so far! ⚠️

Please let me know if this does/does not work for you.

Click and drag the link below to your bookmarks bar in your desktop browser:

ByeTwitterBots

If you're not sure how, this page on start.me explains it clearly - but just drag the "ByeTwitterBots" link on this page instead of the image example they use.

When you're on your notifications page, click the ByeTwitterBots "bookmarklet" in the bookmarks bar. It will report and block every tweet that meets all these criteria:
  • Mentions more than 4 people
  • Is from an account that is following less than 100 people, and followed by less than 100 people
  • Is from an account that you do not follow
Happy blocking!
